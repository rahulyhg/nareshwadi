How to get to Nareshwadi Learning Centre?

By road: Take the Western Express Highway out of Mumbai. Continue on the National Highway number 8. About 20 kms North of Charoti Toll, turn to the left/west at Dundhalwadi, following the sign to Dahanu.  2 km after the turn off, you will see a stone compound wall on your right. You are at Nareshwadi!

By train: Take a suburban/express train from Churchgate, Mumbai Central, Dadar or Bandra stations to Dahanu Road Station. Then take a local bus or shared rickshaw and ask for Nareshwadi, near Dundhalwadi, 26 km from Dahanu. Nareshwadi will be on your left.
